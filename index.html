Pulse

A pre-language interface layer for humans and intelligent systems.

Pulse is a deep-tech foundation layer that enables interaction with digital systems through touch, vibration, and sensory feedback — before language.

Modern technology assumes literacy, language, and learned interfaces. Pulse introduces a missing layer: intuitive, pre-learned interaction that allows systems to be understood and taught through feeling rather than instruction.

Why Pulse

Over a billion people remain excluded from modern systems — not because of access, but because interaction models have not evolved. Pulse reframes interaction itself, creating infrastructure that scales across age, ability, and intelligence.

For Investors

Pulse is not an application or feature. It is a standard-level interface layer designed to embed across OEM devices, AI systems, and future human-machine environments.

The opportunity mirrors prior platform shifts: GUI, touch, voice. Pulse extends this progression by enabling systems to be understood before language exists.
